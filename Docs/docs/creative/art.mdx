## Exploring the Artistic Imagination of Open Source Gaming!

Game development is not just a pile of code and algorithms, but an expression of artistic creation. In the Dora SSR open-source community, we not only focus on the development of technical tools but also cherish every burst of creativity.

Here, we gather developers, designers, and artists who love to create, exploring the intersection of games and art together. From the community's mascots, illustrations, and music to unique peripheral designs, each piece of work carries our passion and imagination.

**SSR Creatives** serves as the community's creative showcase, presenting these creations and providing a platform for inspiration and sharing for every game art enthusiast. Whether it's hand-drawn character designs, visual interpretations of game worldviews, or light and pleasant original music, we look forward to exploring more possibilities of open-source games with you.

:::tip Join Us
Welcome to join us and create the artistic chapter of the Dora SSR community together!
:::

## Character Design

:::info Character Design
**Dora (多萝)** is the mascot of the Dora SSR open-source game engine. Her design is inspired by the protagonist of "The Wizard of Oz", but with a more energetic and exploratory spirit to showcase the spirit of open source. She is a partner to developers, with a calm personality but a brave explorer of the code world, accompanying every creator to start their own fantasy game journey!
:::

:::info Character Design
**Toto (托托)** is a big, friendly dog who sometimes seems to be a bit clumsy. He runs around without thinking, often getting himself and Dora into unexpected adventures. He may not understand code, but he has a natural instinct and a lot of enthusiasm, often bringing unexpected inspiration to Dora at critical moments.
