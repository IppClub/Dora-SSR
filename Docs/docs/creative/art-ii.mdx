## Three Companions, Three Personalities, Three Extensions of Imagination

Just like Dorothy in *The Wizard of Oz* wasn't alone on her journey, our Dora SSR character world gives her three equally unique companions: **Sera**, **Tinie**, and **Rola**.

Inspired by classic archetypes yet reimagined through the lens of game development and open-source culture, these characters are entirely original, collaboratively crafted by our community. Each one is more than just a character design—they represent distinct personalities, symbolic meanings, and vivid expressions of creative imagination.

---

## Character Designs (Continued)

:::info Character Design
**Sera** is a straw girl with golden eyes, her left eye hidden behind her hair, and stitches running along her right arm and left leg. She wears a patchy beige linen outfit and a colorful grain-themed hair accessory. Her movements are graceful, exuding quiet resilience. Sera embodies the spirit of "wisdom in repair"—not born gifted, but constantly learning and reflecting through effort. With her nimble four straw fingers, she weaves through lines of code like braiding threads—calm, meticulous, and steady as the breeze in the fields.
:::

:::info Character Design
**Tinie** is a bio-mechanical girl clad in soft purple and silvery-gray armor. A pixel-style black-and-white heart glows on her chest. Whenever she stares off into space with her wide eyes, the metallic streaks beneath them seem to whisper, “I don’t fully understand human emotions... but I’m working on it!”
She tirelessly works in the background to optimize engine performance, a silent guardian of the code world and the most reliable bug exterminator for developers. Though she looks a bit clueless at first glance, she’s powered by an unwavering core that says: “Not a single frame shall drop!”
:::

:::info Character Design
**Rola** is a bold and spirited young lion prince. He's always the first to charge into action and the life of every community event! Wearing a lion-ear headband and eyes full of adventurous sparkle, he's never afraid to try something new. Rola is a master of gameplay experimentation, the idea factory behind countless open-source games, and the fearless “first tester” of all kinds of wild mechanics.
:::
