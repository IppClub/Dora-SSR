## From Candidate Designs to New Companions

These two mascots began as candidate designs for the Lion (Rola) and the Tin Girl (Tinie). As the character world evolved, their distinct looks and personalities revealed possibilities of their own.

They have now stepped beyond their original concept roles and officially joined the Dora SSR community as new companions. What started as an exploration has become another branch of our shared character world—a reminder that every creative draft can lead to an unexpected new story.
